# Vinkura AI - Operational Intelligence for India

## Mission
To bridge the gap between fragmented data and decisive action in India's public systems. We build the infrastructure that turns scattered records into operational intelligence for law enforcement, district administration, and state departments.

## Visual Identity (Post-Revamp)

### Logo Style
- **Status**: Updated.
- **Aesthetic**: Ultra-thin (`font-weight: 300`), all-caps "VINKURA".
- **Color**: Black on light backgrounds, White on dark backgrounds.
- **Spacing**: Increased letter-spacing (`4px`) for a premium, architectural feel.

### Color Palette
- **High Contrast**: Strict alternation between Deep Black (`#000000`) and Stark White (`#FFFFFF`).
- **Muted Accents**: Use of `#fafafa` and `#f0f0f0` for subtle section boundaries.
- **Typography**: Focused on readability for operational contexts.

## Component Documentation

### Navbar
- **Pattern**: Floating Glassmorphism.
- **Interactive**: Mega-dropdowns for products/company.
- **UX**: Hamburger hidden on desktop. Body-scroll lock implemented for mobile menus.

### Hero
- Focused on "Operational Intelligence".
- Minimalist background to keep focus on the core value proposition.

### Deployment Carousel (FeaturedSection)
- Showcases real-world impact (e.g., Amarnath Yatra, Bareilly Police).
- Uses auto-playing slides with high-res tactical imagery.

### Software Architecture
- **Vinkura Core**: The operating layer.
- **Vinkura Sentinel**: The investigation engine.
- **Vinkura Forge**: Workflow AI.
- **Vinkura Orbit**: On-prem/Edge deployment.
- **Specific Solutions**: DDMS, E-Maalkhana, E-HRMS.

## Design Philosophy
- **Anti-Demo**: We prioritize "Software for real operations" over "AI for demos".
- **Silo-Breaking**: Design patterns should emphasize connectivity and interoperability.
- **Sovereign Data**: All UI/UX flows should reinforce that the user (Government) maintains full control and ownership of data.
